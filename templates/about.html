Welcome to Our College

Vidya Pratishthan Kamalnayan Bajaj Institute of Engineering and Technology, Baramati, is an autonomous institute approved by AICTE and affiliated to SPPU, Pune. We are committed to providing quality technical education, promoting research, and shaping our students into responsible, skilled professionals.

Our Mission

To empower students with technical knowledge and practical skills to excel in the engineering industry and contribute positively to society.

Our Vision

To become a center of excellence in engineering education, fostering innovation, research, and holistic development of students.

Highlights

  • Autonomous status recognized by AICTE.
  • State-of-the-art laboratories and modern campus facilities.
  • Regular seminars, workshops, and technical events.
  • Strong alumni network and industry collaborations.
